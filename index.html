Marin Bougainville Apartment

Welcome to Marin Bougainville Apartment, your cozy retreat in Sarandë, Albania. Our modern one-bedroom apartment offers comfort and convenience, just steps away from Maestral Beach and VIP Beach. With amenities like air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, and complimentary WiFi, we ensure a comfortable stay. Whether you're here to relax by the sea or explore Sarandë's vibrant attractions, Marin Bougainville Apartment is your ideal home base.
