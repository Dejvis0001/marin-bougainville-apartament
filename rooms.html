Marin Bougainville Apartament
One-Bedroom Apartment

Enjoy a comfortable stay in this cozy 30 m² apartment in Sarandë. The apartment features a spacious balcony, free WiFi, air conditioning, and a flat-screen TV. It offers one bedroom with a double bed and is entirely non-smoking.

Located just 300 meters from Maestral Beach and within walking distance of VIP and La Petite beaches, the apartment is also close to cafes and restaurants. With easy access to Corfu International Airport (29 km) and Ioannina Airport (70 km), it’s perfect for a convenient and relaxing getaway. No credit card needed for booking.
